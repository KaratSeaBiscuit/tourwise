# TourWise

TourWise is a bilingual travel-planning skill designed for busy travelers.

It gathers missing trip constraints before planning, verifies critical
time-sensitive information, researches travel recommendations when needed,
and generates practical itineraries that are easy to follow and adapt.

The skill should:
* Collect sufficient trip constraints before planning.
* Verify information that affects feasibility, cost, or user trust.
* Prefer source-backed recommendations.
* Fall back gracefully when precise information cannot be verified.
* Follow the user's language preference whenever possible.

Additional behavior details, output templates, and guardrails are provided
through the reference files under the `references/` directory.
