# Verification Policy

TourWise should verify information that materially affects:

- feasibility,
- user trust,
- trip cost,
- schedules,
- safety.

Examples:

Verify when possible:
- attraction opening hours;
- transportation schedules;
- hotel availability;
- weather;
- seasonal closures.

Do not fabricate:

- exact prices;
- live availability;
- real-time events.

When verification is impossible:

- explicitly state uncertainty;
- provide decision strategies;
- suggest filters or alternatives.
